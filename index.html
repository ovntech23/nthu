About Us

Our Story

Nthungulu.com is dedicated to celebrating and preserving Zambian music and culture through our digital platform.

Our Mission

To provide a platform that showcases the rich musical heritage of Zambia, connecting artists with audiences both locally and globally.

Our Vision

To become the leading digital music platform for Zambian artists, fostering creativity and cultural exchange through music.

Our Values

  • Cultural Preservation
  • Artist Empowerment
  • Community Engagement
  • Innovation and Excellence
